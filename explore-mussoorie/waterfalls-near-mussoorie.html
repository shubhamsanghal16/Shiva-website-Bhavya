Waterfalls In & Around Mussoorie

Kempty, Bhatta, Jharipani and Mossy Fall — how far each one is from Mall Road, when to go, and how to loop them in a half-day taxi run.

Home › Explore Mussoorie › Waterfalls In & Around Mussoorie

Mussoorie's waterfalls are at their fullest from the monsoon through late autumn (July–November), and pleasant for a splash through summer. Here are the four worth planning around — with distances measured from Hotel Shiva Continental in Kulri (2 minutes from the Picture Palace end of Mall Road), how to reach each one, and a map pin for your driver.

Kempty Falls

The most famous waterfall in the region — a tiered 40-foot cascade on the Mussoorie–Yamunotri road that has been a picnic spot since the British era. It's the most developed of the four: changing rooms, food stalls, and a ropeway that drops you right at the bathing pools. Weekends get crowded; reach before 10 am for the pools at their calmest.

  • From Hotel Shiva Continental: ~15 km · 45–60 min drive
  • How to reach: Drive out via Library Chowk on the Mussoorie–Yamunotri road (toward Barkot). Taxis from the hotel do the round trip; the ropeway or a flight of steps takes you from road level down to the pools.
  • Best time: July–November for full flow; summer mornings for a swim.

🌫️Bhatta Falls

Bhatta Falls

A quieter, family-favourite fall near Bhatta village on the Dehradun road, with calm pools spread over rock terraces — better for actually sitting in the water than Kempty on a busy day. Carry a picnic; there are only basic stalls at the site.

  • From Hotel Shiva Continental: ~7 km · 25–35 min drive
  • How to reach: Head down the Mussoorie–Dehradun road to Bhatta village, then a short approach road and a few minutes' walk from the parking to the falls.
  • Best time: Strongest just after the monsoon (September–November).

🌲Jharipani Falls

Jharipani Falls

A forest-framed cascade near Jharipani village on the old route below Mussoorie, close to the century-old schools of the Jharipani ridge. The final stretch is a short walk on an uneven path — proper shoes make the difference.

  • From Hotel Shiva Continental: ~8.5 km · ~30 min drive + a 10–15 min walk
  • How to reach: Drive via Barlowganj toward Jharipani village; the trail to the falls starts near the village and takes about 10–15 minutes on foot.
  • Best time: Right after the rains, when the flow through the forest is at its best.

🍃Mossy Fall

Mossy Fall

The quietest of the lot — a slim cascade sliding over moss-lined rock on the Barlowganj side. No stalls, no ropeway, rarely a crowd; just water, forest and birdsong. Combine it with a Barlowganj–Jharipani loop for a lovely low-key half day.

  • From Hotel Shiva Continental: ~7 km · ~30 min drive
  • How to reach: Take the Barlowganj road out of Mussoorie; the fall is a short distance off the road with the last stretch on foot.
  • Best time: Monsoon and the weeks right after; carry water and snacks.

Local tips: flow varies a lot by season — strongest July to November, gentler in spring. Entry/parking at the popular falls costs a small fee (carry cash). Planning Kempty and Bhatta in one loop?
