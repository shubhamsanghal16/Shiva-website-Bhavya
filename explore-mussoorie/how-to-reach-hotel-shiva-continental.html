Getting Here

How To Reach Hotel Shiva Continental

From the airport, the railway station, the Delhi highway or the bus stand five minutes away — every route to our door in Kulri, 2 minutes from Mall Road.

Home › Explore Mussoorie › How To Reach Hotel Shiva Continental

Where exactly we are

Hotel Shiva Continental sits in Kulri, at the eastern (Picture Palace) end of Mall Road — a 2-minute walk from the Mall Road bustle, on the quieter road that climbs toward Landour. Address: Kulri, Mall Road, Mussoorie, Uttarakhand 248179.

By air — Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun

The nearest airport is Jolly Grant (DED), roughly 55–60 km away — about a 2-hour drive up the hill. Prepaid taxis are available at the airport; ask reception in advance and we'll help arrange a pickup.

By train — Dehradun railway station

Dehradun is the nearest railhead, about 33–35 km from Mussoorie (1.5–2 hours by road). Taxis and shared cabs run from just outside the station through the day; buses to Mussoorie leave from the adjacent stand.

By road — from Delhi and Dehradun

Delhi to Mussoorie is roughly 290 km — typically 6–7 hours via Dehradun on the Delhi–Dehradun highway. From Dehradun, the 33 km hill climb takes about 1.5 hours. The Mussoorie bus stand (Picture Palace side) is about a 5-minute walk from the hotel — the most convenient arrival point for bus travellers and student groups.

The last two minutes

During peak hours parts of Mall Road are closed to vehicles. Arrive from the Picture Palace / Kulri side and you'll reach us without entering the restricted stretch. Hotel parking is limited — please confirm parking in advance; there is also a paid public parking about 4 minutes away, and we offer taxi support throughout your stay. The lift serves floors up to the 5th (Economy Rooms on floors 6–7 are a short flight of stairs beyond).

Need help en route? Call +91 70422 88289 or WhatsApp +91 70605 02380 — the front desk will talk your driver in. Distances are approximate and season traffic on the Dehradun–Mussoorie climb varies.
