Chapter One — The Origin

It began
in 1985.

In 1985, I. C. Sanghal set out to make a mark on Indian hospitality. With his brothers V. K. and R. K. Sanghal beside him, a single hillside property opened its doors in Mussoorie — the first of the Shiva Group, which would grow across Dehradun, Gurgaon and Neemrana.

The mountains were the beginning. They still are.

Chapter Two — The Philosophy

More than rooms.
We offer soul.

Set on a quiet hillside two minutes from Mall Road, our home holds 37 cosy rooms, valley-facing light, and a garden grown from things the world threw away. We were never built to be a place you check into — we were built to be a place you belong to.

Every stay leaves a mark on us as much as on you.
