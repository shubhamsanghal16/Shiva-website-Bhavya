Our Belief

Atithi Devo
Bhava.

"The guest is divine."

It is the oldest idea in Indian hospitality — that whoever arrives at your door arrives as something sacred. We built Shiva Continental around it. Not a hotel that hosts you, but a home that honours you.

The Origin · 1985

A vision, set
in the hills.

In 1985, I. C. Sanghal set out to make a mark on Indian hospitality. With his brothers V. K. and R. K. Sanghal, the first Shiva property opened its doors in Mussoorie — the seed of a group that would grow across Dehradun, Gurgaon and Neemrana. Four decades on, the mountains are still where our story begins.
