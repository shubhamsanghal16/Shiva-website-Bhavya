Getting Here
  • By Car Take NH 707A from Dehradun (33 km). Our hotel is on Mall Road, Kulri, look for the yellow-and-teal signage.
  • By Rail Nearest station: Dehradun (35 km). Taxis available at the station, about 1 hr 15 min to Mussoorie.
  • By Air Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (55 km). Cab transfers can be arranged, contact us before your arrival.
