Deodar Kitchen

Comfort Comes Cooked Here
Our Restaurant

At Deodar Kitchen, we serve meals that feel like home. Picture freshly stuffed parathas with tangy pickle, warm bowls of rajma-chawal, and hot cups of chai to match the mountain air.

Our menu brings together North Indian favourites and familiar Chinese dishes, cooked fresh every day. Whether you're a parent feeding little ones, a couple craving warmth, or a solo guest wanting something comforting, this is your place to pause, eat, and feel looked after.

Ideal For
  • Families looking for hearty, familiar meals
  • Guests who believe food is part of the journey
  • Travellers who prefer soulful, homestyle cooking
What You'll Find at Our Table
  • Breakfast buffet
  • North Indian & Chinese dishes
  • Fresh, local ingredients
  • In-room dining available
  • Tea/coffee available throughout the day
  • Baby chair available on request
  • Comfort food, served warm and simple
