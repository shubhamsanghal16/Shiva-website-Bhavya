Economy Room

Simple Stays, Gentle Days
Home › Rooms › Economy Room

Simple, neat, and thoughtfully arranged, our 80 sq. ft. Economy Rooms are perfect for travellers who need just the essentials. A comfortable bed, clean interiors, and all the basics you need to rest after a day in the hills. Ideal for short stays or budget-conscious guests who want a peaceful night near Mall Road.

Ideal For
  • Solo travellers or couples
  • Guests comfortable with a short stair walk — Economy Rooms are on floors 6–7, beyond the lift's last stop on the 5th floor
  • Budget-conscious guests
  • Short stays with no frills
