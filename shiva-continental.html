Good To Know

Planning a Mussoorie Trip? Quick Answers

Where is Hotel Shiva Continental located in Mussoorie?

In Kulri on Mall Road, Mussoorie — a 2-minute walk from the Mall Road bustle and Picture Palace, on the quiet road that leads toward Landour. Full address: Kulri, Mall Road, Mussoorie, Uttarakhand 248179.

Is Hotel Shiva Continental good for families with children?

Yes. Family rooms like the Suite With Balcony and Super Deluxe With Balcony sleep 2 adults + 2 children, the Deodar Kitchen restaurant has a baby chair, and extra mattresses and board games are available on request. There's also an indoor recreation area (table tennis, carrom) and the Gardenica upcycled garden that kids love.

Does the hotel host student and school groups?

Yes — the hotel has been family-run since 1989, with over three decades of hosting student and family groups in Mussoorie. There's a group buffet option at Deodar Kitchen, an indoor play zone with TT and board games, lift access to all floors, easy bus access, and a dedicated bookings team from pre-arrival to checkout.

Do travel agents get special B2B rates?

Yes. Hotel Shiva Continental is a preferred partner for travel agents across North India, offering competitive B2B rates with a firm rate-parity commitment (OTAs are not allowed to undercut your rates), GST invoicing and real-time WhatsApp coordination. Message +91 70605 02380 with travel dates, room count and guest type to get rates within minutes.

Is breakfast included with the rooms?

Yes, complimentary breakfast is included. Deodar Kitchen, the in-house multi-cuisine restaurant, serves homestyle North Indian and Chinese food, tea and coffee through the day, and in-room dining.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Limited parking is available — please confirm in advance. The hotel also offers taxi support, and there is lift access to all floors.
