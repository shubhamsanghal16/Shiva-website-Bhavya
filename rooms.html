Good To Know

Choosing a Room? Quick Answers

Which room is best for a family of four in Mussoorie?

The Suite With Balcony (300 sq.ft., with a separate living area and hill-facing balcony) or the Super Deluxe With Balcony (240 sq.ft.) — both are designed for 2 adults + 2 children and include complimentary breakfast.

What is the most budget-friendly room at Shiva Continental?

The Economy Room — 80 sq.ft. with a comfortable double bed, clean interiors, complimentary breakfast and Wi-Fi. Ideal for solo travellers, couples and budget-conscious student groups near Mall Road.

Do the rooms have balconies and mountain views?

The Suite With Balcony and Super Deluxe With Balcony open to the Mussoorie hills. The Executive and Deluxe With Balcony rooms have private balconies without a full view (the Deluxe has a partial peek into the hills), and the Deluxe Non View and Economy focus on quiet comfort.

Is breakfast included with every room?

Yes — every room category includes complimentary breakfast, a tea & coffee maker replenished daily, mineral water, Wi-Fi and room service.

Can we book multiple rooms for a group trip to Mussoorie?

Yes. With 6 room categories from Economy to Suite, the hotel regularly hosts student and family groups. For group or B2B bookings, message +91 70605 02380 on WhatsApp with your dates, room count and guest type — GST invoices are provided.
